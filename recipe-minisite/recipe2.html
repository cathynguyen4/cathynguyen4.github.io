Garlic Butter Shrimp

Garlic Butter Shrimp is simple, rich, and full of flavor. It works well on its own or served over rice or pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Pat the shrimp dry and season them lightly with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook for 1 to 2 minutes per side until pink. Remove from the pan.
  4. Add the rest of the butter and the minced garlic to the pan. Stir for about 30 seconds.
  5. Add lemon juice and stir everything together.
  6. Return the shrimp to the pan and toss until coated in the garlic butter sauce.
  7. Top with parsley and serve immediately.
