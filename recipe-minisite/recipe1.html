Shrimp Scampi

Light and flavorful Shrimp Scampi that can be paired with angel hair pasta. Use raw shrimp for best results.

Ingredients

  • ¼ to ⅓ lb of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2–3 cloves of minced garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • White wine

Directions

  1. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook shrimp in olive oil and some butter over medium-high heat for 1–2 minutes per side until just pink. Remove shrimp from the pan.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, add remaining butter, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Cook until fragrant, about 30–60 seconds.
  4. Add white wine and lemon juice, simmer for 2–3 minutes, scraping up browned bits, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Optional: Stir in parsley and serve immediately with extra lemon.
