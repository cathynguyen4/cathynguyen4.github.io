Shrimp Tacos

Shrimp tacos are fresh, flavorful, and easy to put together for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Small tortillas
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Lime wedges
  • Sour cream or taco sauce

Directions

  1. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook the shrimp in a skillet over medium-high heat for 1 to 2 minutes per side until pink.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Fill each tortilla with shrimp and shredded cabbage.
  5. Top with sour cream or taco sauce.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos before serving.
