Pacific Rhododendron

About the plant

Common alternate names

  • Coast rhododendron
  • California rhododendron

Physical size

Typically grows about 3–15 feet tall (can get larger in forest shade).

Geographic range

Found from southern British Columbia through western Washington and Oregon into northern California.

Best climate / growing conditions

Grows best in mild, moist climates (often coastal/forest areas) with acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade.
