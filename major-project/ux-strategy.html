UX Strategy

Planning before design

UX strategy focuses on audience, goals, and purpose before deciding how the final site will look.

What strategy asks first

Before designing a fashion website, it helps to think about who the site is for and what those users need. A strategy page should ask: What is the main goal of the site? Is it meant to sell products, build a brand, or show creative work? What should users find first when they arrive?

Planning early keeps the site more focused and prevents random pages or features from being added without a clear reason.

User goals

Browse products, find inspiration, learn about the brand, compare options, and feel confident using the site.

Brand goals

Build trust, create a strong identity, guide users toward important pages, and keep the experience consistent.
