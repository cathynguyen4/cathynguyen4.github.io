Examples

Good vs. bad fashion website choices

Comparing stronger and weaker choices can make UX ideas easier to understand.

Stronger choices

  • Simple homepage hierarchy
  • Readable text over images
  • Consistent buttons and page labels
  • Obvious contact or help section

Weaker choices

  • Overcrowded landing page
  • Too many fonts or colors
  • Hidden information users need
  • Layouts that break on mobile
