UX Scope

Choosing the right content and features

Scope is about deciding what belongs on the site and what should be left out to keep it useful and clear.

Must-have content

Homepage, about page, clear navigation, contact area, brand information, and key pages users expect.

Helpful features

Organized menus, readable layouts, mobile-friendly structure, product details, and easy ways to move through the site.

What to avoid

Too many extra sections, cluttered layouts, repetitive content, or features that look nice but do not help the user.
